Multidimensional Poverty
in Ogun State

The Nigeria MPI 2022 shows that about br 7 in 10 (68.1%) people in Ogun State - 3.7 million - are multidimensionally poor. On average, the multidimensionally poor in Ogun State experience 42.4% of the weighted deprivations that make up Nigeria's MPI.

A significant proportion of this poor population are deprived of adequate sanitation, clean cooking fuel, and healthcare.

Health related deprivations significantly contribute to multidimensional poverty in Ogun State

Over 6 in 9 people in Ogun State are multidimensionally poor and deprived of improved sanitation facilities

Unimproved sanitation facilities include: flush to somewhere else or unknown place (not sewer system, septic tank, or pit (latrine)); pit latrine without slab; bucket; hanging toilet or latrine; and no or other non-improved sanitation facility.
